Sony A7IV Portrait Indoor Artificial Settings

Exact settings for Sony A7IV portrait photography indoors under artificial light.
Recommended Mode Aperture Priority Shutter Speed 1/125s Aperture f/1.4–f/2.0 ISO Auto, cap at 6400 Focus Mode Face and Eye AF Drive Mode Single Shot White Balance Tungsten

The Key Setting

For indoor artificial light portraits, the Sony A7IV's AF performance remains excellent, but the real challenge is colour. Tungsten and LED lighting can introduce a warm, orange cast that throws off skin tones. The A7IV's Auto White Balance does a decent job, but switching to the Tungsten preset (3200K) gives a cleaner starting point. If you're shooting in mixed lighting — a window plus warm lamps — set Kelvin to around 4500–5000K to balance the two sources. Enable Zebra Display set to 100+ to avoid blowing out highlight details on the subject's skin, especially when using a fast aperture like f/1.4.
