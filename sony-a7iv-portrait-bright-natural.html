Exact settings for Sony A7IV portrait photography in bright natural light.

The Key Setting

In bright natural light, the A7IV's 33‑megapixel sensor rewards careful composition. The extra resolution over 24‑megapixel bodies means you can crop into a portrait without losing texture, which is valuable when the subject is backlit and you want to tighten the frame around the face while keeping the warm rim light intact. Enable Zebra Display set to 100+ to warn you before highlights clip to pure white; the A7IV's dynamic range can recover highlights, but you want to avoid losing texture in the brightest areas of the subject's skin.
