Sony A7IV Event Indoor Artificial Settings

Exact settings for Sony A7IV event photography indoors under artificial light.
Recommended Mode Manual + Auto ISO Shutter Speed 1/500s Aperture f/2.8 (groups) / f/1.4–f/2 (singles) ISO Auto, cap at 3200 Focus Mode Face and Eye AF Drive Mode Continuous High White Balance Tungsten

The Key Setting

The A7IV's AF Subject Shift Sensitivity is critical for events. Set it to 1 (Locked On) for tracking a specific subject through a crowd — the camera will resist jumping to other faces. Set it to 5 (Responsive) for quickly switching between different subjects as the scene changes. Assign "Subject Shift Sensitivity" to a custom button or the Fn menu so you can toggle between these two modes instantly as the situation demands. Enable "Silent Shooting" for ceremonies; the A7IV's electronic shutter is fast enough for most event work without rolling shutter distortion.
